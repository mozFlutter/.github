# MozFlutter Community

Welcome to **MozFlutter**, the Flutter community of Mozambique.

This repository serves as a **central hub** for the community, containing information about who we are, how to participate, active projects, and how to contribute.

## What is MozFlutter?

**MozFlutter** is an open community of Flutter developers, students, and enthusiasts in Mozambique, created to:

- Share knowledge about Flutter and Dart
- Connect developers, students, and companies
- Build practical and real-world projects
- Promote events, workshops, and meetups
- Support new members in the mobile ecosystem

Everyone is welcome, regardless of experience level.

## How to Participate

1. Star the community repositories
2. Join the official channels
3. Explore the available projects
4. Choose an issue marked as a `good first issue`
5. Contribute via **Fork + Pull Request**
