## Our Commitment

**MozFlutter** is committed to maintaining an **open, inclusive, respectful, and safe** community for everyone, regardless of:

- Experience
- Gender
- Origin
- Age
- Nationality
- Technical opinions

## Expected Behaviors

- Mutual respect
- Clear and constructive communication
- Openness to learning and teaching
- Healthy collaboration
- Empathy with other members

## Unacceptable Behaviors

- Offensive or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks
- Harassment or intimidation
- Toxic or disrespectful comments
- Any behavior that harms the community

## Accountability

MozFlutter directors have the right and responsibility to:

- Moderate content
- Remove inappropriate contributions
- Take necessary actions to protect the community

## Reporting

If you witness or experience any inappropriate behavior, please contact the Community administrators through official channels.

## Final

MozFlutter exists to **learn, grow, and build together**.

By being part of the community, you help make it better for everyone.
